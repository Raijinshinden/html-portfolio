The Best Movies in Ryan's Opinion

My Top 3 Movie Recommendations

1. Killing Machine/ Shorinji Kempo

This Sonny Chiba film recounts the life of Doshin Sou, the founder of Shorinji Kempo.
The title was changed to Killing Machine for American audiences.

2. Wolf Children

A beautifully animated movie about a woman who has to single handidly raise her children after her husbands untimely death.

3. Black Dynamite

A hilarious Blaxploitation parody.
